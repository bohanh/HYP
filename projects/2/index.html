Safe Haven

Safe Haven

A Sanctuary for Survivors of Domestic Violence

Marco Lanza

Read more

Safe Haven is a collaborative initiative uniting local government agencies, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and community organizations with a common goal: to provide safe housing and comprehensive support services to survivors of domestic violence. This project not only ensures a secure environment for survivors but also focuses on training law enforcement and healthcare professionals to better identify and respond to cases of gender-based violence.
